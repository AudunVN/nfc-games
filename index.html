Fursuits - Gotta Catch 'Em AllJoin now!

The success from last year is back! This is a fursuit catching game in which three factions compete with each other to “catch” the most amount of fursuits during the convention!

Catching fursuits is as simple as meeting a fursuit, and asking them for their catch code printed onto their fursuit badge. Enter the code into the website or app, and blamo - Your catch has been registered!

Only fursuits with blue badges are part of the game.

Achievement HuntingJoin now!

Have fun collecting various achievements around the convention, then view the ones you’ve collected so far in the handy NFC app so that you can brag to your friends! This year, achievements are primarily code based. If you find a code hidden anywhere, make sure to bring it to the wise and all knowing Huehuecoyotl (The Blip Master) so that it may be presented to the Gods. Rumour has it that a shipment of gold coins have also been lost around the convention area... not sure what that is all about, but if you find one, maybe the Huehuecoyotl would be interested in that as well.

There's a list of known achievements in the conbook, but the convention area is vast and mysterious, so who knows if there are additional achievements hidden away somewhere? Be observant and curious, and you may discover the hidden secrets of the convention!

Mausie's LabPlay now!

Help Mausie eat cheese and avoid the evil clones of Trax in this NFC themed version of one of the oldest arcade games. To play the game via Telegram, ask the NFC info bot for a game in any channel by typing "@nfcinfobot game" and selecting it in the list.

If you play the game directly in the browser without Telegram, your highscore won't be saved anywhere...
